# Data Sources and Attribution

This file documents the provenance, license, and download instructions for all datasets used in this analysis.

## Included in this repository

These files are small enough for GitHub and have redistribution-compatible terms.

- **Source:** https://www.onetcenter.org/database.html
- **Files:** `db_29_1_text.zip` (13MB, included)

### BLS OEWS (data/oes/)

- **License:** U.S. public domain (17 U.S.C. 105)
- **Attribution:** Source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics.
- **Source:** https://www.bls.gov/oes/tables.htm

### Felten et al. AIOE (data/ai-exposure/)

- **License:** No explicit license. Data published on public GitHub repo with citation requirement.
- **Attribution:** Felten E, Raj M, Seamans R (2021). Occupational, industry, and geographic exposure to artificial intelligence: A novel dataset and its potential uses. *Strategic Management Journal* 42(12):2195-2217.
- **Source:** https://github.com/AIOE-Data/AIOE
- **Files:** `AIOE_DataAppendix.xlsx`, `Language_Modeling_AIOE_AIIE.xlsx`, `Image_Generation_AIOE_AIIE.xlsx`

- **License:** U.S. public domain (17 U.S.C. 105)

### BEA (data/bea/)

- **License:** U.S. public domain
- **Attribution:** Source: U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis.
- **Source:** https://www.bea.gov/

### Autor-Dorn task measures (data/autor-dorn/)

- **License:** No explicit license. Authors request citation only.

### Intermediate analysis files (data/analysis/)

- Generated by scripts in `analysis/`. These are derived from the sources above.

## NOT included — download separately

These files are too large for GitHub (>100MB each). They are U.S. government public domain data — free to download, no restrictions.

### CPS ASEC microdata (~1.4GB)

Download from: https://www.census.gov/data/datasets/time-series/demo/cps/cps-asec.html

Place files in `data/cps-asec/csv/`:
- `asecpub19csv.zip` through `asecpub25csv.zip`
- Extract 2024: `data/cps-asec/csv/2024/pppub24.csv`

Attribution: Source: U.S. Census Bureau, Current Population Survey, Annual Social and Economic Supplement.

### ATUS microdata (~1.5GB)

Download from: https://www.bls.gov/tus/data/datafiles-0324.htm

Place files in `data/atus/`:
- `atuscps_0324.dat`, `atusresp_0324.dat`, `atussum_0324.dat`

Attribution: Source: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, American Time Use Survey.

### ABS microdata (~570MB)

Download from: https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/abs/data.html

Place files in `data/abs/`:
- `ABSCB2019.dat`, `AB2300CSCB01.dat`

Attribution: Source: U.S. Census Bureau, Annual Business Survey.
